What is climate change?

  • Climate change occurs when changes in Earth's climate system result in new weather patterns that remain in place for an extended period of time. In this case, global warmig is catching up to us due to the amount of human beings who do not care about trashing their environment.
  • Climate change can alter where species live, how they interact, and the timing of biological events, which could fundamentally transform current ecosystems and food webs.
  • It can overwhelm the capacity of ecosystems to shift to extreme scenarios, such as wildfires, floods, and dorughts.
